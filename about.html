üî¨ Research Methodology

Our research follows a structured Experiential Learning procedure tailored for Urban Mobility optimization:


1. Gap Analysis

Identifying limitations in existing Indian driving cycles which fail to account for high traffic density.

2. Data Acquisition

Collection of high-fidelity GPS logs from the ISCON-PDPU bus corridor to ensure realistic modeling.

3. Model Synthesis

Application of MMAS for path construction and ABC for local fuel optimization.
